About

Ayana and Cana

Hello, and welcome to our website! Thank you so much for visiting! We are Ayana and Cana, two first-years at Harvard College from Tokyo, Japan, and this is our final project for CS50. Both of us love reading, writing, and literature in general, and hope you will enjoy browsing through the content on this site. Please feel free to reach out to us at agathaslibrary@gmail.com or consider submitting an original piece of your own for this website by going to the Contact page.

Agatha's Library

We named this site "Agatha's Library" after Mary Shelley's character Agatha De Lacey from Frankenstein; or, the Modern Prometheus. In the novel, Agatha and her family are secluded or "quarantined" in a cabin in Germany and the one bright spot of their lives is when Agatha's older brother, Felix De Lacey, reads aloud. In the same spirit, we hope you will find a story or essay on our site that sparks a little bit of joy for you!

Thank you to Leonie Finke, a friend of the creators', for designing our logo!
